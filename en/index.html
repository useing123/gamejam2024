C:/satbayev/dev.hub/about

«SU Game Jam 2021» from the Institute of Automation and Information Technology, Satbayev University and the Hub community is a four-day competition from independent game developers from all over the Republic dedicated to the creation of games! The event will be held offline, and works are accepted only from those who participated on the official part. «SU Game Jam 2021» will be held in team mode, so come with your friends or find a team right on the game jam!

    Only for you:
  • Consultation of professional specialists
  • Useful workshops and lectures from professionals
  • Game development in 48 hours in a creative environment
  • 43 prizes and 4 nominations to win
  • Interesting acquaintances
  • Demonstration of your game to a wide audience of gamers

And also, everything that will make these 4 days unforgettable: new friends, community support, crazy ideas, an incredible boost of your skills, last-minute filling and passing games from many other developers of the country! The goal of «SU Game Jam 2021» is to build a strong community of game developers and popularize the game development industry in our country! See you soon!

C:/satbayev/dev.hub/faq

+ What should I do if I don't have a team?

- You can apply to participate alone, either assemble a team on site, or join already existing teams before the start of development. However, if you fail to find the command, you will not be able to take part in the competition, but you will be able to take part in lectures and workshops.

+ Our team has no experience at all, what to do?

- «SU Game Jam 2021» is a great way to get your first experience, because everything happens for the first time!

+ Can we use Unity3D/RPG MAker/Game Maker or any other game engine in the development of our project?

- Yes, you can use any game engine if the final project will be available on the platforms specified in the rules of «SU Game Jam 2021».

+ The project must necessarily correspond to the theme of «Retro»?

- Yes. This is a necessary limitation to encourage your creativity.

+ Do we need to bring our own equipment (Laptops, graph. tablets, etc.)?

- Yes, you need to come to the festival «SU Game Jam 2021» with your own equipment. BYOD (bring your own device) format.

C:/satbayev/dev.hub/rules

  • The project should correspond to the theme of «SU Game Jam 2021», in this case – «Retro». However, the interpretation of the topic remains with the participants
  • Teams have only 48 hours to develop the games
  • Development Platform - PC (Windows/Linux/macOS systems) or Web
  • Mandatory attendance at registration and closing of the event.
  • At least 2 and at most 5 people in the team
  • It is allowed to use blanks or previously created prototypes, if they were created by participants
  • It is allowed to use any development technologies
  • It is allowed to use any development technologies Games should be based on a theme, and also in the project there should be things that refer to 3 of the 6 specified additional topics (topics are revealed immediately before the start of «SU Game Jam 2021”)
  • It is allowed to use all publicly available libraries and middleware
  • All utilities for creating content and utilities for developers (3dsmax, Photoshop, Blender, etc.) are allowed
  • He source code, a compiled file and a team report (Word, PowerPoint, etc.) with the argumentation of the project must be attached to the game.
  • The copyright holders of the games developed within the framework of the «SU Game Jam 2021» festival are the Organizer and the General Sponsor.
  • Team interaction is unlimited.
